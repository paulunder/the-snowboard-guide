Beginner

What should you buy as a beginner?

First of all it is important, that you are sure you wanna start a new sport, which needs a lot effort in the beginning. If you are not sure, go and rent your gear and try it. After some renting sessions you can be sure you wanna do this and can buy gear without headache.

BOOTS

Snowboard Boots have some different values that are important for you as a beginner.

BOOT-Size

Your shoe size should fit, if your toes hit a little bit the front of the boot, then it is ok. If it feels a little bit loose, then consider another shoe size or even another lacing system - f.e. BOA.

BOOT-Flex

As a beginner you should choose a soft flexing boot. This will help you to learn the right technique and you will have more fun. If you are a little bit heavier, you can choose a little bit stiffer boot.
